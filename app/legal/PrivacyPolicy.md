# Privacy Policy — NIA (Sovereign Living Assistant)
**Effective Date:** 2026-03-01 | **Compliant with:** DPDP Act (India, 2023), GDPR (EU)

---

## 1. Our Privacy Philosophy: Sovereign Data
NIA is built on a **Local-First** architecture. Your data is your sovereign property. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data.

## 2. What We Collect (On-Device Only)

| Data Type | Where Stored | Retained |
|---|---|---|
| Voice commands | RAM (session-only) | Never persisted |
| Screen pixel data | RAM (session-only) | Never persisted |
| Task history | Local encrypted DB | User-controlled |
| Preferences & config | Local JSON | User-controlled |

## 3. What We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** transmit voice or screen data off-device.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
- We do **not** create advertising profiles.

## 4. Cloud Sync (Optional)
If you opt-in to the **Empire Tier** cloud backup:
- Data is encrypted client-side (AES-256) before transmission.
- Only encrypted blobs are stored. Codurra Labs cannot read your data.
- You may delete your cloud backup at any time from Settings.

## 5. Your Rights (DPDP 2023 / GDPR)
- **Right to Access**: Request a full export of your local NIA data.
- **Right to Erasure**: Delete all data from Settings > Nuke Data.
- **Right to Portability**: Export your data in JSON format.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Disable Sovereign Mode or uninstall NIA.

## 6. Security
- Local data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest.
- Sovereign Mode actions require explicit user authorization.
- NIA never stores API keys in plaintext.

## 7. Children's Privacy
NIA is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

For privacy concerns: **privacy@codurralabs.in**
